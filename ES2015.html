1. What is the difference between var and let?
  2. What is the difference between var and const?
  3. What is the difference between let and const?
  4. What is hoisting?

  1. While you can declare and reassign with var, with let you can only reassign. With let, you can also create a block scope.
  2. You cannot reassign or redeclare with const, while with var you can. Const creats a block scope.
  3. You can reassign with let, but you cannot reassign with const.
  4. Has to do with how javascript is compiled. Variables are brought to the top of the scope they're declared in. Var is the only one that can be moved to the top before being declared later on.
